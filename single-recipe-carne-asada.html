Carne Asada

This easy carne asada recipe is as simple as can be—fire up the grill and you’ll be feasting on juicy carne asada tacos in no time.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Whisk the orange juice, lime juice, olive oil, garlic, oregano, chili powder, salt, cumin, pepper and cayenne in a small bowl.

step 2

Place the steak in a 9x13 baking dish or gallon size ziplock bag and cover it with the marinade until fully coated. Refrigerate for 1 to 4 hours.

step 3

Preheat the grill medium-high heat (400-450°F).

step 4

Discard the extra marinade and season steak with salt and pepper.

step 5

Grill the steaks for 5-8 minutes per side, depending on the thickness.

step 6

Remove steaks and allow to rest for 5 minutes. Slice thinly against the grain and serve.

ingredients

2 lbs flank or skirt steak

1 orange juiced (1/4 cup)

2 limes juiced (1/4 cup)

2 tbsp oil (olive or vegetable), plus more for grill

8 garlic cloves, minced (2 tbsp)

2 tsp oregano

2 tsp chilli powder

2 tsp kosher salt, plus more for steak

2 tsp cumin

1 tsp freshly ground pepper, plus more for steak

1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

tools

A baking dish or Ziploc bag.

A grill.

A small mixing bowl.

Grilling tongs are very helpful.

An instant-read thermometer.
